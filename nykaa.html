Nykaa Fashion — monetizing browsing intent

Nykaa has a monetization problem, not a traffic problem. Fixing the operational trust gap at the decision layer can unlock ₹113–225 Cr in annual GMV without acquiring a single new user.

The core insight

Users trust Nykaa for inspiration but not for transaction. The platform excels at discovery but fails at the decision layer due to fit uncertainty and return anxiety, forcing users to discover on Nykaa but purchase on competitor platforms.

What was working
  • ·22.2M monthly visitors driven by a strong creator network
  • ·Superior editorial curation capturing high discovery intent
  • ·Healthy top-of-funnel engagement and wishlist saving
What was broken
  • ·62% of users face fit uncertainty with no size-specific social proof
  • ·Hostile post-purchase return ecosystem traps money and destroys trust
  • ·19 million users exit at the moment of payment due to operational fears
Research & Method
1
Online survey — 29 participants

Identified primary e-commerce pain points. 82% rated return policies as highly important; 75% expressed frustration with post-purchase fit issues.

2
In-depth interviews — 5 users

Explored qualitative shopping behaviors. Confirmed that users treat Nykaa as a catalog and execute purchases on Myntra due to better return reliability.

3
Voice of the customer — 100+ interactions

Analyzed Reddit, LinkedIn, and App Store reviews. Uncovered systematic operational failures in the return loop and a measurable collapse of post-purchase trust.

4
Quantitative funnel analysis

Benchmarked SimilarWeb and FY25 investor data to map session depth, bounce rates, and pinpoint the 85.6% drop-off concentrated at the PDP and checkout stages.

Solution / Output
Solution 1
UGC-powered fit intelligence layer

Real customer photos and fit ratings surfaced on every product page. Addresses the fit void by providing size-specific social proof, directly converting users stalled by pre-purchase fit anxiety.

8M reach RICE 640 2 person-months AI data foundation
Solution 2
Return experience redesign + Nykaa Promise

A 15-day no-questions return guarantee on owned brands, backed by a one-tap, Nykaa-managed pickup flow. Fixes the operational failure and signals trust directly at the decision layer.

7M reach RICE 980 7 person-months Phase 1 launch
