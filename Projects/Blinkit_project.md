Analysis & Recommendation

Don't build a bigger catalog. Build a trusted destination.

## Agent Build Notes

### Critical reminders for the AI builder

1. **Never introduce cool grays.** Every neutral must have a yellow-brown undertone. Use `#5e5d59`, `#87867f`, `#4d4c48` — never `#666666`, `#94a3b8`, or any blue-gray.
2. **All serif headings at weight 500.** No 600, no 700, no 400 on the serif font. The single-weight consistency is the voice.
3. **Page background is always `Parchment #f5f4ed`** on light sections. Never pure white. Cards are `Ivory #faf9f5`.
4. **Use ring shadows for interaction depth, not drop shadows.** Pattern: `0px 0px 0px 1px #e8e6dc` or `0px 0px 0px 1px #d1cfc5`. Whisper shadow when elevation is needed: `rgba(0,0,0,0.05) 0px 4px 24px`.
5. **Terracotta `#c96442` is reserved for primary CTAs, pull-quote left borders, pillar number badges, and the highest-signal brand moments.** Do not spray it across body text or section labels.
6. **Tables need breathing room.** 14–18px vertical padding per row. `Border Cream #f0eee6` horizontal dividers only. No zebra stripes.
7. **Dark sections appear at:** Section 3 (Core Insight), Section 5 (Market Sizing), Section 9 (Analysis & Recommendation), Section 12 (Closing Reflection). All other sections are Parchment.
8. **Body line-height ≥ 1.60** on all prose. This is a reading experience, not a dashboard.
9. **Competitor matrix in Section 7** — build as SVG or CSS-based 2×2. No external chart libraries needed. Use dashed borders and subtle fill for the "space to capture" zone. Terracotta dot for Blinkit target position.
10. **Stat callout numbers** use Serif 52px weight 500. On dark sections, use `Coral Accent #d97757` for the highest-signal stat (SOM in Section 5). On light sections, use `Near Black #141413`.
11. **Pull-quote blocks** use `4px solid Terracotta #c96442` left border. Ivory background. 24px radius. Left-align text — never centered inside pull-quotes.
12. **No decorative lines, accent bars, or AI-style header underlines.** Section separation comes from background color shifts and whitespace only.
13. **Force rating badges** in Section 6 — use `Warm Sand #e8e6dc` pills, 24px radius, Sans 12px Charcoal Warm. One pill per card in the top-right corner.

### Responsive behavior
- Desktop (992px+): multi-column grids, full tables, 64px hero headline.
- Tablet (768–991px): 2-column grids, condensed tables with horizontal scroll container.
- Mobile (<768px): single-column stack, 36px hero headline, hamburger nav. Tables scroll horizontally inside a `Warm Sand #e8e6dc` container.
